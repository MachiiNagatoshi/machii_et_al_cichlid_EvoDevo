# machii_et_al_cichlid_EvoDevo

Title:Mosaic of evolutionary origins underlies parallel evolution of hypertrophied lips in East African cichlids

We analyzed the genetic and developmental basis of hypertrophied lips, one of the representative examples of parallel evolution across cichlids in East African Great Lakes. Integration of genome-wide scans, time-series transcriptomics and scRNA-seq demonstrates that multiple genes under natural selection collectively assembled hypertrophied lips.
